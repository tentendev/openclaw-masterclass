## Video Learning Tips

1. **Adjust playback speed**: Use 1.5x-2x for familiar content
2. **Watch and do**: Have your terminal ready and follow along
3. **Take notes**: Record commands, config values, and important caveats
4. **Rewatch**: Complex architecture explanations deserve at least two viewings
5. **Join the community**: Discuss unclear parts on [Discord](https://discord.gg/openclaw)
